Executive Committee

Meet our dedicated leaders

Narayan Prasad Dhakal

President

Shri Narayan Prasad Dhakal, Chairperson of Neeti Anusandhan Pratishthan, Nepal (NeNAP). He is an original social researcher. He has special interest in the study of religion, culture, tradition and philosophy, and has keen interest in social and educational research. He has kept himself engaged in several bilateral initiatives aimed at consolidation Nepal-Bharat relations by developing environments for better understanding and dialogue. Narayan Prasad Dhakal has, in tandem with the organizational works, developed cordial relations with all political organizations, nation bearing institutions, government machineries, academicians, diplomats, intellectuals, opinion makers, media, etc. in all social and national walks of life. Narayan Prasad Dhakal has been successful in popularizing the beliefs that these three can and should be accommodated as members of the same family. It is one of his most significant contributions that he showed, over the years that teachers, educationists and students can co-work in the same organization at different capacities, though students constitute the most significant component. He has played a crucial role in expanding the organization from campuses to towns and districts, consolidating the organizational work in national proportion, balancing organizational and programmatic activities and developing a long network of workers with enhanced skills and potentials.

Dr. Prem Raj Neupane

Vice-President

Associate Profesor Dr. Premraj Neupane has completed his PhD in Buddhism from Lumbini Buddhist University and working in Nepal Sanskrit University, Kathmandu. He has published several articles on Buddhism and other philosophies and presented papers in national and international conferences. Also, he is involved on different social organizations.

Dipendra Kumar Bhatta

General Secretary

Mr. Dipendra Kr. Bhatta is a graduate of B.COM (Honours) from Delhi University, India. He graduated in the year 1998 and then went to England for higher studies in Accounting & Finance. Thereafter, he worked in the UK for some years, before finally returning to his home country Nepal in the year 2006. Further to his return, he worked in some of the top corporate houses in Nepal and also started business ventures on his own. Besides pursuing his business endeavours, Mr. Bhatta is actively involved in Social Services. He has been associated with NeNAP since the year 2018.

Pravesh Acharya CA.

Secretary

Pravesh Acharya is a Certified Chartered Accountant currently based in Nepal. He has completed his Chartered Accountancy from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, New Delhi in 2017. He has the wide range of expertise in the arena of Finance, Taxation, Foreign Direct Investment, Accountancy and Law. As an expert, he had served in Fiscal Budget and Commercial Law promulgation process in Finance Committee of Federal Parliament. He is serving as a consultant in the largest corporate houses, Government and Non Governmental Organizations. As an passionate advocate of Tax and Fiscal Justice, he is serving as national treasurer of Tax and Fiscal Justice Alliance Network. He is famous for his prowess in Tax Related Laws of both Nepal and India.
